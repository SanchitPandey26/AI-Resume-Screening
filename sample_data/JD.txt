Engineering Internship Innovate (An overview)

Innovate is looking for motivated and detail-oriented Engineering Interns to join our engineering teams. During this internship, you will gain hands-on experience across different stages of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including:

Software Development
Quality Engineering (QA)
DevOps
Release Management
At Innovate, we believe engineers should understand how products are built end-to-end. This internship will give you exposure to real projects, modern technologies, and cross-functional collaboration from development to deployment.

You will work in at least one of the roles listed above, and depending on team needs, you may get the opportunity to contribute across multiple areas. This approach reflects the evolving industry trend where traditional role boundaries between developer, QA, and DevOps are becoming more integrated.

Our goal is to help you build strong technical foundations, practical problem-solving skills, and a holistic understanding of how engineering teams deliver real-world products.

What You'll Do

AI Software Development

Design, develop, and maintain software applications and scripts in collaboration with cross-functional engineering teams.
Translate user requirements into technical specifications and contribute to architecture discussions.
Participate in live project and team discussions, completing assigned tasks on time.
Document software functionalities and development processes.
Complete a final internship project under close mentorship.
Quality Engineering (QA Automation)

Assist in designing, developing, and maintaining automated test scripts for various product features.
Identify test cases suitable for automation in collaboration with the QE team.
Execute automated test cases, analyze results, and report defects.
Enhance and optimize existing test automation frameworks.
Participate in code reviews and contribute to improving coding standards and best practices.
Document test plans, test cases, and test results clearly and accurately.
DevOps

Exposure to multi-cloud environments (AWS, Azure, GCP) within the Innovate infrastructure.
Assist in the development and maintaining of CI/CD pipelines using ArgoCD and Jenkins to streamline deployment, testing, and operational workflows .
Implement and manage infrastructure as code using tools like Terraform or Crossplane .
Contribute to creating, updating, and optimizing Helm charts for Kubernetes-based application deployments.
Write and maintain Python scripts for automation tasks related to system administration and deployment.
Monitor system performance, troubleshoot cloud environment issues, and implement solutions to improve reliability and efficiency.
Support the optimization and automation of Linux-based tasks.
Release Management

Manage release cycles and emergency bug fixes to streamline and automate software delivery pipelines.
Assist in designing, implementing, and maintaining CI/CD workflows to support a growing business.
Develop and implement tools and solutions for migration and acceptance testing.
Assist in maintaining scalable and reliable platform infrastructure.
Support security and privacy standards to meet certification requirements.
Identify and act on opportunities to improve and simplify infrastructure for a better customer experience.
